Since the Beginning

A Dream Poured Into Every Cup

We started in 2012 as a tiny cart on the corner of Maple Street. Our dream was simple: to serve coffee that was not just a morning fuel, but a moment of pause, a sensory experience, and an open invitation to connect with neighbors.

Over the years, we expanded into our full roastery and cafe, but our foundation remains unchanged. We source our coffee beans through Direct Trade contracts, roast in small batches, and train our baristas like classical musicians.

"We started with a simple belief: coffee should bring people together and make their day a bit brighter. Every bean tells the story of the farmer who nurtured it, the roaster who crafted it, and the barista who poured it."

— Sophia, Founder & Head Roaster

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Ethical Sourcing

We pay premiums above Fair Trade prices directly to farmers, ensuring sustainability for agricultural families and absolute transparency.

Absolute Quality

From sorting raw cherries to testing mineral profiles in our water, we obsess over every minute detail to deliver the perfect pour.

Community First

Our cafe is a sanctuary. We host open mics, art exhibitions, and coding sessions, dedicating our resources to support local initiatives.

Constant Innovation

We constantly experiment with fermentation methods, nitro brews, and seasonal syrups, pushing boundaries while respecting tradition.

Warm Hospitality

We welcome everyone with an authentic, friendly smile. Here, you are not just a customer, you are an honored guest.

Always Learning

We conduct regular barista workshops and public cupping sessions to share the knowledge and science of coffee extraction.

Milestones

Twelve Years in the Making

2012

The First Seed

Sophia opens the first Brewed & Co. mobile cart in Brooklyn, serving single-origin filter drip coffee to busy morning commuters.

2015

Roasting In-House

We acquire our first 5kg commercial roaster, taking complete control over our beans' flavor profiles and roasting profiles.

2018

The Flagship Cafe

Brewed & Co. moves into our permanent home on Maple Street, converting a historic brick warehouse into our cozy flag-ship cafe.

2020

Pivot and Delivery

During challenging times, we launch our Direct-to-Home subscription boxes, keeping our neighborhood fueled with fresh coffee bags.

2022

The Farm Direct Program

Sophia travels to Central and South America to establish Direct Trade contracts, building direct relationships with our farming partners.

2024

Looking Ahead

Introducing our zero-emission roasting process and expanding our barista training school for local youth programs.

Direct Trade

Where Our Beans
Come From

We do not buy from bulk auctions. Instead, we sign long-term purchasing agreements directly with family estates in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Sumatra. By cutting out middle-men, we pay up to 40% more to farming communities, enabling them to build schools and fund sustainable water projects.
